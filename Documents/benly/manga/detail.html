BERSEK

4.9 Action, Seinen, Dark Fantasy Kentaro Miura

Synopsis

Guts, a former mercenary now known as the "Black Swordsman," is out for revenge. After a tumultuous childhood, he finally finds someone he respects and believes he can trust, only to have everything fall apart when this person sacrifices everything he's ever known for his own selfish desires. Now, Guts is marked for death and hunted by demons, but he will not stop until he has exacted his revenge.
